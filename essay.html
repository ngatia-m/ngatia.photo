Documenting the world, one frame at a time.

Muthoni Ngatia is a documentary and street photographer drawn to the unguarded moments that reveal the texture of everyday life. Her work takes her across continents — from the vibrant fish markets of Mumbai and the narrow alleys of Zanzibar, to the colourful street scenes of Latin America and the wide skies of Angola.

She approaches her subjects with patience and curiosity, seeking the intersection of colour, light, and quiet human drama. Her photographs are as much about the spaces between people as they are about the people themselves — the glance, the gesture, the pause before the moment passes.
